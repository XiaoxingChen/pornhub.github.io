--- title: About me date: 2020-04-04 11:10:11 ---

Xiaoxing (pronounced "shaw shin") Chen is a software engineer at ARM Mixed Reality Group. His work interests spans the range of autonomous driving, robot mapping and visual odometry. Prior to joining ARM, Chen was an Intern at BWM Autonomous Driving Lab. Chen earned his M.S. in Electronic Science and Technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and holds a B.E. in Mechatronics from Zhejiang University. In 2014-2015, Chen was a member of RoboCup Small Size League Team at Zhejiang University.
