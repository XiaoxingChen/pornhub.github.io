About Me

I am Xiaoxing (pronounced as "Shawsheen") Chen, a software engineer at the Effect Department of ByteDance Ltd. My work interests spans the range of robotics, multiview geometry and graphics. Prior to joining Bytedance, I worked at Arm China Shanghai Campus. The experience includes a SLAM/AR project on Arm platform and a ray tracing project on Mali GPU with vulkan API. I was an Intern at BWM Autonomous Driving Lab in 2018. I earned my Master's degree in Electronic Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and holds a B.E. from Zhejiang University. In 2014-2015, I was a member of RoboCup Small Size League Team at Zhejiang University.
